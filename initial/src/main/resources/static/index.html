Telestrations!

Telestrations or "Eat Poop You Cat" is a game combining the classic games:
Pictionary and Telephone

Players will write a phrase or draw a picture to pass to the next player. The next player will then do the opposite and pass their creation to the next player. Play continues until the specified limit and produces hilarious results!
